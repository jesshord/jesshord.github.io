A5: Getting Meshy!

By Jessica Hord

Documentation

I really wanted to make a planter for one of the plants on my desk, so I used this as an opportunity to make one.

I found two different planters I wanted to combine together.

I used the MeshBooleanSplit tool and planes to cut off the pieces i didn't want.

After that, I just put the legs where I wanted them and cleaned up the connect points using the same methods. After it was ready, I did the "Join" feature and created one closed mesh.

I printed it!

Also, for this assignment, I worked on my lamp.
